04 · Local highlights

What we have here

A non-exhaustive tour of the planet's headline amenities.

🌊

Oceans

Five interconnected liquid-water bodies, average depth 3.7 km. Home to whales — gentle giants that sing across entire seas.

🏔️

Mountains & forests

Peaks up to 8,849 m, rainforests that make their own weather, and an aurora light show at both poles. All natural, no subscription.

🎶

Music

Humans arrange air vibrations into patterns that cause involuntary joy. Works on most carbon-based nervous systems.

🍕

Food

From street noodles to wood-fired pizza, humans turned survival into an art form. Warning: highly habit-forming.

🐕

Dogs

Quadruped companions who decided, ~20,000 years ago, to love humans unconditionally. They will probably love you too.

💡

Ideas

Mathematics, poetry, spacecraft, the internet. Humans are loud and chaotic, but occasionally brilliant.

Park your craft responsibly. Oceans are scenic but corrosive; deserts have free long-term parking.

Do not abduct the cows. They are someone's cows.

TIP-04

Try the pizza. Then the mangoes. Then the dosa. Trust this transmission.

TIP-05

TIP-06

If a small human waves at your ship, wave back. It will make their entire year.

06 · The golden record

We already sent you a mixtape
